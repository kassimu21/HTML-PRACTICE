Sir Ahmadu Bello

The First and only Premier of Nigeria

Here's a timeline of Sir Ahmadu Bello's life:

  • 1910 - Sir Ahmadu Bello was born in Rabah into the family of Mallam Ibrahim Bello.
  • 1931 - He finished school and became an English master teacher in Sokoto middle school.
  • 1934 - Sir Ahmadu Bello was made the District Head of Rabah by Sultan Hassan dan Muazu, succeeding his brother.
  • 1938 - Made an attempt to become the Sultan of Sokoto but was not successful, losing to Sir Siddiq Abubakar III who reigned for 50 years until his death in 1988.
  • 1944 - He was back at the palace to work as the Chief Secretary of the State Native Administration.
  • 1952 - Sir Ahmadu Bello won a seat in the Northern House of Assembly.
  • 1954 - Bello became the first Premier of Northern Nigeria.
  • 1959 - Bello led the NPC to win a plurality of the parliamentary seats.
  • 1966 - He died.
